LEARN ABOUT THE BRAIN

1. Amazing Brain Size: Your brain is super powerful! It's like a computer inside your head, and it's the control center of your body.

2. Gray and White Matter: Your brain has two main parts: gray matter and white matter. Gray matter helps you think and remember, while white matter connects different parts of your brain so they can talk to each other.

3. Brain Messages: Your brain uses special messengers called neurons to send messages. They're like tiny talkers that pass information around your brain.

4. Brain Connections: Neurons make connections like a huge web. The more you learn, play, and explore, the stronger these connections become!

5. Left and Right Sides: Your brain has two sides, called hemispheres. The left side helps with math and language, while the right side is great for creativity and art.

6. Memory Marvels: Your brain can store lots of memories. From fun times with friends to learning new skills, it keeps these memories safe for you to remember later.

7. Sensational Senses: Your brain helps you see, hear, smell, taste, and touch the world around you. It's like a fantastic coordinator for your senses!

8. Dream Machine: Ever wonder why you dream at night? Your brain is like a movie maker, creating cool stories while you sleep.

9. Brain Exercise: Just like your body, your brain needs exercise too! Reading, puzzles, and games keep your brain sharp and ready for action.

10. Keep It Safe: Wearing helmets and protecting your head is important because your brain is precious and needs to be kept safe!

Remember, your brain is an extraordinary part of who you are. Treat it well, challenge it with new things, and it will help you explore the world and discover amazing things!
